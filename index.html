Program Sections

The hockey club program section focuses on developing skills, teamwork, and sportsmanship. It offers training, competitions, and community events,
fostering a love for the game while promoting healthy lifestyles and camaraderie among players of all ages and skill levels.

Junior Program

The junior hockey club program focuses on skill development, teamwork, and sportsmanship. Players learn the fundamentals of the game through training sessions and friendly competitions, fostering a love for hockey while promoting physical fitness and lasting friendships among teammates.

Teenager Program

The Teenager Program offers engaging activities that promote personal growth, leadership, and social skills. Participants explore interests through workshops, sports, and community service, fostering friendships and self-confidence while preparing for future challenges in a supportive and fun environment.

Professional Program

The professional hockey program is designed for aspiring elite players, focusing on advanced skills, tactical training, and physical conditioning. Participants receive expert coaching, compete at high levels, and gain exposure to scouts and recruiters. The program emphasizes teamwork, discipline, and sportsmanship, preparing athletes for successful careers in hockey and personal development beyond the ice.
